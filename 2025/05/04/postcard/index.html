My Postcrossing Life

Recently,I’ve exchanged postcards on instagram and facebook,here is my outgoing.I just created an Instagram account yesterday to connect with people for postcard exchanges. However, after sending a few DMs, my account was suddenly restricted. I immediately submitted an appeal and anxiously waited, assuming I might lose the account for good. To my surprise, it was restored within an hour! The experience made me cautious—I’ll avoid aggressive liking or messaging for now.

Work has been quiet today, with little on my plate. I considered sourcing some products, but many items are hard to find reliable Chinese suppliers for, as they’re rarely manufactured locally.

This week, I’m excited to attend a collaborative postcard meet-up activity in Chengdu for my first time! I look forward to meeting fellow enthusiasts and writing postcards together.
